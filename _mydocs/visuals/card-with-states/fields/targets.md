The ***Targets*** field well accepts one or more measures used as comparison targets for the cards.

If you do not bind a target, the [Reference](../options/data-label/index.md#reference) label is not available.

When ***Category*** is used, ***Targets*** accepts a single measure. When ***Category*** is empty, you can bind multiple target measures, but they must match the number and order of the measures bound to ***Values***.
