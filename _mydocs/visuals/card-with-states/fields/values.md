The ***Values*** field well is required and accepts one or more measures to display in the cards.

If you bind more than one measure, Card with States renders multiple cards and the ***Category*** field well is not available.

When ***Category*** is empty, multiple ***Targets*** measures can be bound and are matched to the ***Values*** measures according to their order.
