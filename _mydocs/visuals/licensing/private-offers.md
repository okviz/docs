When you purchase a license on AppSource, the transaction is handled entirely by Microsoft: **the invoice is issued by Microsoft and the payment is made to Microsoft**, which generally accepts credit cards only. OKVIZ is not part of the transaction and cannot issue an invoice or accept a payment for a purchase made on AppSource.

This is particularly relevant for **Synoptic Panel Lite**: because it is a [certified visual](../certification.md), it is available exclusively through [AppSource Licensing](appsource.md) and cannot be purchased from the OKVIZ website.

If your organization requires an invoice issued by OKVIZ, the standard option is to purchase through [OKVIZ Licensing](okviz/index.md) on our website. Keep in mind that visuals licensed by OKVIZ [cannot be certified by Microsoft](../certification.md).

## Private Offers for Enterprise Agreements

For large enterprise volumes, OKVIZ can arrange a **private offer**: a custom agreement that results in the visual being downloaded from AppSource — therefore **certified** — and fully licensed, while invoicing is handled directly with OKVIZ.

## Eligibility

Creating and managing a private offer is a complex and time-consuming process that involves manual work on both sides. For this reason, **private offers are reserved for enterprise agreements that meet a minimum contract value**, and are evaluated case by case at OKVIZ's discretion. They are not available for standard or small-volume purchases.

If your purchase does not qualify for a private offer, you can:

- Purchase on AppSource with the standard checkout (invoicing and payment managed by Microsoft).
- Purchase through the [OKVIZ website](okviz/index.md) (invoice issued by OKVIZ, but the visual is not certified).

If your organization is planning an enterprise agreement and wants to explore this option, please [contact our administrative support team](mailto:backoffice@okviz.com).
