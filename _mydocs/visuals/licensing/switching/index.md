If you want to switch an OKVIZ visual from one licensing system to another, there is **no direct switching path**.
AppSource Licensing and OKVIZ Licensing are managed separately, so the subscription cannot be converted automatically.

In practice, every switch follows the same rule:

1. Cancel the current subscription in the original licensing system.
2. Create a new subscription in the target licensing system.
3. Replace the visual in your reports with the package or AppSource version from the new licensing system.

## Before You Start

Before you proceed, keep the following points in mind:

- The two subscriptions are independent.
- Billing history, license state, and assignments are not transferred from one system to the other.
- Switching the subscription does not automatically replace the visual already used in your existing reports.
- To avoid service interruptions, it is best to plan the switch close to the end of the current billing period.
- If you move to AppSource Licensing, remember that AppSource works with per-user assignment.

## Choose the Switching Path

Select the guide that matches your current subscription:

- [AppSource → OKVIZ](switch-appsource-to-okviz.md), if your current subscription is managed by Microsoft and you want to move to a direct OKVIZ subscription.
- [OKVIZ → AppSource](switch-okviz-to-appsource.md), if your current subscription is managed directly by OKVIZ and you want to move to Microsoft AppSource.

> If you are not sure which licensing system is best for your scenario, start from the [Licensing Overview](../index.md).
