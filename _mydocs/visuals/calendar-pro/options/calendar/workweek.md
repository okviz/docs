**Default value:** *Automatic* (based on current [localization](../localization/index.md))

This option allows you to decide how to visualize the workweek in the visual.

Available options:

- **Monday - Friday**
- **Monday - Saturday**
- **Saturday - Wednesday**
- **Saturday - Thursday**
- **Sunday - Thursday**
- **Sunday - Friday**
