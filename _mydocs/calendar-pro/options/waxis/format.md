**Default value:** (Auto)

This option defines how weeks are displayed.

Choose among different the following formats:
- **Week of month**.
- **Week of quarter**.
- **Week of year**.
- **ISO Week**.
- **ISO Week + year**.
- **Days**.
- **Short range**.
- **Range**.
